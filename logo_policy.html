Regulations for the use of the logo

  • Your organization may want to use the ARL Logo to market itself as a company registered to ISO certificates. Artwork is supplied on computer disk/print to assist you or your printer. Where certificates issued to you is accredited one from the accreditation board for the scope of your registration, you may use either the basic ARL Logo, or the Logo combined with the relevant accreditation body Accreditation Mark to which your scope of registration relate. However, please observe the following, which will be subject to monitoring by ARL during surveillance visits.
  • Conditions of Use:

    a) Logos may not be used on Products or Packaging or other materials which may suggest product approval.
    b) Logos may be used on letterheads, business cards, brochures, advertising material.
    c) There is no restriction as to the size of the basic ARL Logo.
    d) If required, place of your organization’s name in the bottom centre of the ARL Logo.
    e) For testing & calibration Laboratories Client shall not use ARL Middle East logo on test results
  • Conditions for the combined ARL/EGAC Accreditation Mark

    a)The combined ARL/Accreditation Mark Logo may be used only by organization whose registration is covered by ARL’ accreditation of that accreditation board.
    b) The Accreditation Mark may be used only with the ARL logo to the left of it. The logo and Accreditation Mark must be enclosed in a box and reproduced accurately, strictly in accordance with the artwork.
    c) The accreditation mark logo shall not be reproduced in isolation of the ARL logo.
    d) The Accreditation Mark may not be affixed to, or appear on, a road vehicle, building, flag or product packaging.The Logo shall not be used in anyway that it misleads the reader about the accredited status of ARL or certified body.
    e) The EGAC Logo may be reproduced, as described in the given link EGAC Procedure3: Rules of Procedure Governing the Use of the Accreditation Symbol
  • If any doubt?

    If you have any doubt regarding the use of logo, please contact the ARL office before printing.
